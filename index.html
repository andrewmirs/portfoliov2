About Me

Ever since the beginning of high school, I have been curious about the art and design of the world around me. What makes something work, how is it functioning at its core, or why are we motivated to make the choices we do. This initially led me to chase a Business Marketing degree at Cal State Fullerton, focusing on product design and market strategies.

About a semester away from graduating, I was introduced to programming and couldn’t shake the feeling that I had to figure out a way to incorporate this into my life. A few months after completing my degree, I found LearningFuze, an accelerated web development program where I’ve since spent thousands of hours on and off-campus learning, practicing and creating all kinds of different applications. I usually keep my hands full, but I am always looking forward to new projects. I would love to discuss any topic related to programming, technology, or design, so feel free to reach out!
